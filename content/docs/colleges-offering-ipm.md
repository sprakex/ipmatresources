The IPM ecosystem has grown a lot since IIM Indore started it. Here's every IIM that offers some form of an integrated management program as of 2026.

---

#### The Main Players (IPM Programs)

| IIM | Exam | Degree | Extra Requirement |
|-----|------|--------|------------------|
| **IIM Indore** | IPMAT Indore | BA (Foundations of Management) + MBA | — |
| **IIM Rohtak** | IPMAT Rohtak | BBA + MBA | — |
| **IIM Ranchi** | JIPMAT | BBA + MBA | — |
| **IIM Bodh Gaya** | JIPMAT | BBA + MBA | — |
| **IIM Jammu** | JIPMAT | BBA + MBA | — |
| **IIM Shillong** | IPMAT Indore | BBA + MBA | Maths in +2 |
| **IIM Amritsar** | IPMAT Indore | B.Sc (Quantitative Finance & Economics) + MBA | Maths in +2 |

A few things to note:
- **IPMAT Indore** is the exam by IIM Indore. Some other IIMs also accept this score (Shillong, Amritsar).
- **JIPMAT** is a separate exam conducted by NTA for Ranchi, Bodh Gaya, and Jammu.
- **IPMAT Rohtak** is its own exam — different paper, different pattern.
- Shillong and Amritsar have a math requirement. If you didn't take math in 11th-12th, those are off the table.

---

#### The Newer Entries (Non-IPM but Integrated)

These IIMs have UG programs too, but they're not branded as IPM. Still worth knowing about.

| IIM | Exam | Degree |
|-----|------|--------|
| **IIM Bangalore** | IIMB-UAT | B.Sc (Hons) Economics / Data Science |
| **IIM Kozhikode** | IIMK-BMS AT | BMS (Honours with Research) |
| **IIM Sirmaur** | IPMAT Indore | BMS |

IIM Bangalore's program is relatively new — you can pick between Economics and Data Science as your major. IIM Kozhikode's BMS is also new. Both are solid options if you're okay with a different entrance exam.

---

#### Which exam should you target?

Here's the rough logic:

- **Want the OG IIM experience?** → Target IPMAT Indore. Also keeps Shillong and Amritsar as backup options.
- **Want to stay closer to home (East)?** → Target JIPMAT for Ranchi or Bodh Gaya.
- **Want a different flavor?** → Check out IIMB-UAT for Bangalore or IIMK-BMS AT for Kozhikode.

You can appear for multiple exams. They don't clash often. Just don't spread yourself too thin.

---

#### A Note on IIM Shillong

Shillong accepts IPMAT Indore scores but has a separate counseling process. You apply to them separately after the results. Their intake is small, so competition is real. But the campus is gorgeous, so there's that.

---

#### A Note on IIM Amritsar

Their program is a B.Sc in Quantitative Finance & Economics + MBA. If you're into finance, quant stuff, or data, this is worth a serious look. Also accepts IPMAT Indore scores.
