title: IIMs offering IPM
description: Indore, Shillong, Ranchi, Jammu, Bodhgaya, Amritsar, Rohtak

___
Following are the colleges which offer **IPM** as of 2026:


| IIM Campus    | Entrance Exam Accepted | Degree Awarded                                |
| ------------- | ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| IIM Indore    | IPMAT Indore           | BA (Foundations of Management) + MBA          |
| IIM Shillong  | IPMAT Indore           | BBA + MBA                                     |
| IIM Ranchi    | IPMAT Indore           | BBA + MBA                                     |
| IIM Jammu     | JIPMAT                 | BBA + MBA                                     |
| IIM Bodh Gaya | JIPMAT                 | BBA + MBA                                     |
| IIM Amritsar  | IPMAT Indore           | B.Sc (Quantitative Finance & Economics) + MBA |
| IIM Rohtak    | IPMAT Rohtak           | BBA + MBA                                     |

Note: IIM Shillong and IIM Amritsar require you to have chosen Mathematics/Applied Mathematics as a subject in your +2.

IIM Bangalore, IIM Kozhikode and IIM Sirmaur do also have undergraduate programmes but not IPM as of 2026.

| IIM Campus    | Entrance Exam Accepted | Degree Awarded                                                   |
| ------------- | ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| IIM Bangalore | IIMB-UAT               | **B.Sc. (Hons) in Economics** (with a minor in Data Science)     |
| IIM Bangalore | IIMB-UAT               | **B.Sc. (Hons) in Data Science** (with a minor in Economics)     |
| IIM Kozhikode | IIMK-BMS AT            | **Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS) - Honours with Research** |
| IIM Sirmaur   | IPMAT Indore           | BMS                                                              |
