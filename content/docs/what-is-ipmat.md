So you're here because someone mentioned IPMAT and now you're down a rabbit hole. Let's fix that.

**IPMAT** = **Integrated Programme in Management Aptitude Test**. It's the entrance exam for the 5-year Integrated Programme in Management (IPM) — basically a direct path from 12th grade to an MBA. Finish school, spend 5 years at an IIM, walk out with a Bachelor's + Master's degree. No separate graduation, no CAT, no gap years.

IIM Indore started this back in 2011 and it's been growing ever since. Now multiple IIMs have jumped on the bandwagon with their own versions.

---

#### The Structure

The IPM is a 5-year course split into two parts:
- **Years 1–3**: Foundation courses — economics, math, statistics, humanities, communication. You build a solid base.
- **Years 4–5**: The actual MBA curriculum, same as what the 2-year PGP folks study.

You graduate with an integrated degree — a BA/BBA/BSc (depending on the IIM) plus an MBA.

---

#### Why do people choose this?

- **Save a year**: UG (3) + MBA (2) = 5 years either way. Doing IPM means you don't waste time figuring out graduation later.
- **No CAT stress**: Your MBA seat is locked if you clear IPMAT and the interview. Everyone else is killing themselves over CAT while you're chilling.
- **IIM tag from day one**: You're an IIM student from the moment you join. Campus life, exposure, peer group — all of it for 5 years.
- **Better ROI**: 5 years at an IIM costs less than a random degree + CAT coaching + 2 years MBA at a decent college.

---

#### The Exam Format

Computer Based Test. 90 questions. 3 sections. 40 minutes each.

| Section | Questions | Type |
|---------|-----------|------|
| Quantitative Ability (Short Answer) | 15 | Type in the answer |
| Quantitative Ability (MCQ) | 30 | Choose one |
| Verbal Ability | 45 | Choose one |

+4 for right, -1 for wrong. But here's the thing — **QA-SA has zero negative marking**. So attempt all 15 even if you have no clue.

---

#### Selection Process

1. **Written Exam** → Your score gets normalized into an ATS (Aptitude Test Score).
2. **Shortlist** → Top ~800 candidates based on ATS get called for PI.
3. **Personal Interview** → The veto round. Can save you or sink you.
4. **Merit List** → 65% weight on ATS, 35% on PI. No class 10th/12th marks considered.

---

#### Eligibility (the short version)

- Minimum 60% in class 10th and 12th (55% for SC/ST/PwD)
- Born on or after a specific date (usually August 1st of the year — check the brochure)
- Appearing for 12th boards in the same year is fine

Check the exact criteria on [IIM Indore's website](https://iimidr.ac.in/programmes/academic-programmes/five-year-integrated-programme-in-management-ipm/ipm-admissions-details/) or use [this tool from AfterBoards](https://www.afterboards.in/ipmat-jipmat-iim-ipm-eligibility).
