___
Integrated Programme in Management Aptitude Test is an Aptitude Test conducted by IIM Indore for their [Five Year Integrated Programme in Management (IPM)](https://iimidr.ac.in/programmes/academic-programmes/five-year-integrated-programme-in-management-ipm/).

#### Eligibility Criteria

You can find the criteria on [IIM Indore's website](https://iimidr.ac.in/programmes/academic-programmes/five-year-integrated-programme-in-management-ipm/ipm-admissions-details/), you can also use [this](https://www.afterboards.in/ipmat-jipmat-iim-ipm-eligibility) tool to check if you're eligible.
#### Aptitude Test Format

It is a Computer Based Test (CBT) consisting of 3 sections:
1. Quantitative Aptitude Short Answer (15 Questions)
2. Quantitative Aptitude Multiple Choice Question (30 Questions)
3. Verbal Ability (45 Questions)
   
Each section has a time limit of 40 minutes, the marking scheme followed is +4/-1 for correct and incorrect answers respectively. There is no negative marking for incorrect answers in the QASA section.
___
#### Computation of Aptitude Test Score (ATS)


| Component                                       | Score (Weight)                                                                                                      |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Score in Quantitative <br>Ability (MCQ) Section | $25*\left[ \frac{\text{Candidate's Score}-\text{Minimum Score}}{\text{Maximum Score}-\text{Minimum Score}} \right]$ |
| Score in Quantitative <br>Ability (SA) Section  | $25*\left[ \frac{\text{Candidate's Score}-\text{Minimum Score}}{\text{Maximum Score}-\text{Minimum Score}} \right]$ |
| Score in Verbal Ability <br>(MCQ) Section       | $50*\left[ \frac{\text{Candidate's Score}-\text{Minimum Score}}{\text{Maximum Score}-\text{Minimum Score}} \right]$ |
| Aptitude Test Score (ATS)                       | $\text{Sum of the above}$                                                                                           |
___
#### Personal Interview

Every year, about ~800 students are shortlisted for the next stage of the admission process, the Personal Interview. The [cutoffs](https://www.afterboards.in/ipmat-jipmat-cutoff) for the shortlisted candidates are released by IIM Indore each year after IPMAT.

Interviews are a **veto** round, they can make or break your preparation and are not to be taken lightly. Use [this](https://lilcrows.notion.site/AfterBoards-Ultimate-Guide-to-IPM-Interviews-a9d8f713ad00422fa36035acdcf49801) (you've probably read this a thousand times) guide to prepare for your interviews.

___

#### Composite Score Calculation

IIM Indore does **not** take your class 10th and 12th marks into consideration *explicitly*.

The weightage is as follows:


| Component                 | Weight |
| ------------------------- | ------ |
| Aptitude Test Score (ATS) | 65%    |
| Personal Interview        | 35%    |
