---
title: Should I give IPMAT?
description: Honest advice on whether IPM is the right call for you
---

This is the million-dollar question, and the honest answer is — it depends. There's no universal yes or no. But here's a framework to help you figure it out.

---

#### You should definitely consider IPM if…

- **You know you want a career in business/management.** Not "maybe" — you're fairly certain. IPM is designed for people who've made up their mind early.
- **You want the IIM tag without the CAT grind.** CAT is a nightmare. 2 lakh+ people fighting for ~5000 seats. IPMAT has way fewer candidates and way better odds.
- **You value time.** 5 years for UG+MBA is efficient. Your peers will be done with graduation and still figuring out their next step while you're already in the MBA phase.
- **You can handle the pressure.** 5 years at an IIM is no vacation. Assignments, projects, deadlines, internships — it's intense. If you're up for it, great.
- **You have the academic backing.** 60%+ in 10th and 12th (or can realistically achieve it). That's the eligibility bar.

---

#### You should think twice if…

- **You're not sure what you want to do.** 5 years is a long commitment. If you might want to switch to engineering, law, design, or something else — keep your options open. A regular 3-year degree gives you more flexibility.
- **You're doing it just because your parents/peers are pushing it.** Classic trap. This is your 5 years, not theirs. Make sure you actually want this.
- **You're weak at math.** Quant is 50% of the exam and the first 3 years involve decent math/stats. If you genuinely hate numbers, IPM will be a struggle.
- **You want to study abroad for UG.** IPM locks you into the Indian system. If you're dreaming of a US/UK/EU undergrad, this isn't the path.
- **Your 10th/12th scores are way below 60%.** Unless there's a dramatic improvement coming, eligibility itself could be an issue.

---

#### The financial reality check

IPM is not cheap. Tuition across 5 years at IIM Indore is roughly ₹25-30 lakhs. Hostel and mess add more. Rohtak and others are in a similar range.

| Item | Approximate Cost |
|------|-----------------|
| Tuition (5 years) | ₹25-35 lakhs |
| Hostel + Mess | ₹5-8 lakhs |
| Total | ₹30-40 lakhs |

That said, most IIMs offer need-based and merit-based scholarships. And the average placement for IPM graduates is solid — ₹25-30 LPA in recent batches. So the ROI works out if you put in the work.

---

#### What if you're still in 11th grade?

Good news — you have time. Focus on:
- Building your math fundamentals (this is your biggest edge)
- Reading regularly (verbal ability is a silent killer)
- Keeping your 12th board percentage above 60-65%

You can start serious prep in 12th. Don't burn out early.

---

#### What if you're in 12th right now?

You're in the right window. IPMAT usually happens in May. JIPMAT follows in June. You can give both.

Your game plan:
1. Lock in your board prep first (eligibility depends on it)
2. Parallel prep for IPMAT — QA and VA in equal measure
3. Register when forms open (usually February-March)
4. Give the exam, give the interview, hope for the best

And if it doesn't work out? There are plenty of other great paths. This is not a do-or-die situation, no matter what anyone tells you.
