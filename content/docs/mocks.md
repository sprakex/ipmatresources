#### Quick tips

- Most of these require a phone number or email signup. It's annoying but standard.
- If you want zero registration, **LPT EdTech** has a PDF-based mock you can download directly.
- **Cracku** and **EduRev** give you the most free content without pushing upsells too hard.
- Don't just spam mocks. Take one, analyze where you're weak, work on those topics, then take another.
- Start giving mocks at least 2-3 months before the exam. Early on, focus on sectionals rather than full-length.
